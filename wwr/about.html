WHITE WATER RAFTING

White Water Rafting was born from a shared passion for adventure and a deep appreciation for the natural wonders of the outdoors. Founded in 1995 by outdoor enthusiasts Jake Thompson and Sarah Reynolds, the company started with humble beginnings, operating out of a small trailer near the banks of the Clearwater River in Idaho.

History

Over the years, White Water Rafting has become synonymous with adventure and exploration, attracting thrill-seekers from around the world to experience the rush of white-water rafting with expert guides. From families seeking unforgettable vacations to corporate groups bonding through team-building adventures, White Water Rafting continues to create memories that last a lifetime

As word spread about the thrilling experiences offered by White Water Rafting, demand for our services grew rapidly. Jake and Sarah seized the opportunity to expand their operations, adding new routes and destinations to their repertoire. They also invested in state-of-the-art equipment and safety protocols, ensuring the highest standards of service for their customers.
