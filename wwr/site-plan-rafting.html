Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site if for rafting enthusiasts, teams, and organizations that want to engage the activity to have an easy way to learn more about the sport, answer questions and book the services provided. Engaging with the audience and keep them updated on the latest news and events related to the sport

Audience

Who are the target customers?: Rafting enthusiasts, teams, and organizations. Over 30 years olds, individual with disposable income. What do they want?: Adventure. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?: How hard or risky the sport is and where do I start. What are their needs that aren't being met? Find a simple site to book their next adventure while answering all questions. How will my audience usually access my site?: Desktop, laptop, and mobile device, on that order
